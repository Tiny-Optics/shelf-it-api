1. GetSlowMovingProducts
Purpose: Identifies products with low activity to help with inventory optimization

Route: POST /api/Admin/GetSlowMovingProducts

Parameters:

frmDaysThreshold (optional): Analysis period in days (default: 30)
frmMaxActivityThreshold (optional): Max activities to consider "slow" (default: 5)
frmLimit (optional): Number of results to return (default: 20)
Features:

Analyzes products based on activity frequency and recency
Calculates activity scores combining frequency and quantity moved
Shows current stock levels for slow-moving items
Identifies days since last activity
Perfect for inventory optimization and identifying dead stock
2. GetStateComparisonReport
Purpose: Comprehensive comparison of performance metrics across all states

Route: POST /api/Admin/GetStateComparisonReport

Parameters:

frmStartDate & frmEndDate (optional): Date range for analysis
Features:

Compares total activity, quantity moved, and unique products/stores per state
Calculates market share percentages for each state
Shows activity density (activity per store) and product diversity
Includes growth rate comparison with previous period
Identifies top-performing states and market leaders
3. GetPredictiveAnalytics
Purpose: Forecasts future demand based on historical patterns using simple trend analysis

Route: POST /api/Admin/GetPredictiveAnalytics

Parameters:

frmForecastDays (optional): Days to forecast ahead (default: 30)
frmHistoricalDays (optional): Historical data period (default: 90)
frmProductLimit (optional): Products to analyze (default: 10)
Features:

Analyzes weekly activity patterns for trend stability
Generates activity forecasts based on historical trends
Provides confidence levels (High/Medium/Low) for predictions
Calculates recommended stock levels with buffer
Shows market-level growth predictions
Identifies seasonal patterns and demand fluctuations
Key Analytics Insights Provided:
Inventory Management:
Slow-moving products for clearance decisions
Recommended stock levels based on demand forecasts
Current vs. predicted stock needs
Market Intelligence:
State-by-state market share analysis
Regional growth trends and opportunities
Product diversity across markets
Strategic Planning:
Demand forecasting for procurement planning
Market expansion opportunities
Regional performance benchmarking
